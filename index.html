Why Hydrogen Cars?

🌍

Zero Emissions

Hydrogen cars only produce water vapor. No CO2, no pollution. Just clean energy for our planet.

⚡

Hydrogen Efficiency

Converts 60-70% of energy into motion. Much better than petrol (20-30%) and competitive with electric cars.

🚀

Future Technology

Hydrogen fuel cells are the next step in transportation. Already used by Toyota, Hyundai, and BMW.

How Hydrogen Cars Work

1

Hydrogen Storage

Hydrogen gas (H₂) is stored in a high-pressure tank (like petrol in a normal car)

2

Fuel Cell

Hydrogen passes through a fuel cell that converts it into electricity

3

Electric Motor

The electricity powers an electric motor that drives the wheels

4

Water Only

The only emission is water vapor. Clean and safe for the environment
